Whack-a-Mole

Rules are simple: whack'em and stack'em!

Directions
  • The object is to hit as many moles as possible
  • Use your mouse pointer to steer the mallet
  • Click your mouse to swing the mallet
  • To begin hit the START button
  • The moles will randomly pop up for 30 seconds
  • Once the timer reaches 0 hit the RESET button
  • Another player can go or you can play again
  • Do your best to set the new high score!
